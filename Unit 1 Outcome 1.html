1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

Information technology has an essential role in business's daily operations and success. Modern business use information technology to increase productivity and efficiency. Information Technology helps businesses to save costs , improve customer experience and to enhance communication . It plays role in helping businesses to expand. When it comes to mange resource weather it is human resource management or other resources, information technology enable business act efficiently. The need for information technology in modern business is the essential thing without which no business can survive in these competitive times.
