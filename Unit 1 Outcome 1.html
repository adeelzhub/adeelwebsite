1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

Information technology has an essential role in business's daily operations and success. Modern business use information technology to increase productivity and efficiency. Information Technology helps businesses to save costs , improve customer experience and to enhance communication . It plays role in helping businesses to expand. When it comes to mange resource weather it is human resource management or other resources, information technology enable business act efficiently. The need for information technology in modern business is the essential thing without which no business can survive in these competitive times.

1.2 Describe methods, skills and resources needed to complete digital projects successfully

Digital Projects like any other project needs planning before its development. Listing resources, selection of IT tools needed to complete the digital project is also part of the planning. Then before the development, the design is created. After planning comes the development stage. This is where the project starts to come to existence. After the development, the project is reviewed and tested and if the test identifies any issues then those are fixed and it is tested again. This is repeated until we get a working product which fulfils the intended purpose. Digital projects need skills to fulfil them. Skills to use tools available for the project development e.g. if a programming language is being used then knowledge of coding in that specific language is essential. To manage the project, project management skill is required. Communication skills and problem-solving skills are also needed. Flowchart help visualize the project before its development. Kanban boards are a good way of managing digital projects and help in keeping track of the progress.

There are legal guidelines which have to stick to while working on digital project. A persons creative work is his property. There are laws and regulation to protect people’s work.
