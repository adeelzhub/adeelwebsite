3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency

There are two ways to increase productivity, work more hours or work smarter. In the begging of the project I was not working smarter I was doing hours, never looked at the time. Fewer breaks or sometimes no breaks at all. but that was not efficient way. Woring smarter is the key to improve productivity and efficiency. Keeping track of the progress and then planning your work accordingly is a productive and efficient approach. Taking regular breaks gives you a fresh start every time. Also something I have learnt from my experience is that one should avoid multitasking while coding as this only causes confusion. It makes you more tired and effects your efficiency. Having minimal intruption also improve the quality of results.
